FAQs

Common questions

Do I need to download an app? ▼
No! FitCoach AI is a Progressive Web App. On Android, you'll see an "Add to Home Screen" prompt automatically. On iPhone, tap the Share button and choose "Add to Home Screen". It installs like a native app, completely free.
How does the calorie tracker work? ▼
Log any food with its calories and macros (protein, carbs, fat). The app shows a visual daily summary with a calorie ring and macro breakdown. Your data is saved on your device.
Can I pay with UPI? ▼
Yes! We accept UPI, debit/credit cards, and net banking through Instamojo — India's most trusted payment platform. Instant activation after payment.
How accurate are the AI plans? ▼
Plans are generated by Claude (Anthropic's AI) and tailored to your exact goal, fitness level, age, and equipment. They follow scientifically sound principles, though we recommend consulting a doctor before starting a new fitness program.
